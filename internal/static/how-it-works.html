4-Step Flow

From US checkout to Caribbean delivery, without guesswork.

Use your personal suite at checkout, monitor every intake event, then ship consolidated packages on your schedule.

Start in 60 Seconds Estimate Cost

Step 1: Create your account and get your suite code

Your suite code links all incoming packages directly to your locker.

Step 2: Shop any US store

Use your QCS address at checkout exactly as provided in your account.

Step 3: We receive, inspect, and store

Each package is weighed and photographed. Storage is free for the first 30 days.

Step 4: Create shipment and dispatch

Select packages, complete declaration details, pay securely, and track your shipment.

Common questions

Is sign-up free?

Yes. You only pay once you are ready to ship.

What stores are supported?

Any US store that can deliver to a US address.

How long is storage free?

30 days free, then daily storage fees apply.
