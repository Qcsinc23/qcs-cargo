How QCS Cargo Works

1. Get Your Address

Sign up in 60 seconds and receive your unique US mailing address with a suite code. Use it at any US online store.

2. Shop Anywhere

Shop at Amazon, eBay, Walmart, or any US retailer. Ship your orders to your QCS Cargo address in New Jersey.

3. We Receive & Store

We receive your packages, photograph and weigh each one, and store them for 30 days free. You get a photo and details in your locker.

4. Ship When Ready

Choose which packages to ship, add customs details, pay, and we forward to Guyana, Jamaica, Trinidad & Tobago, Barbados, or Suriname.

Quick questions

Is sign up free? Yes. You get your address and 30 days free storage.

How long can I store? 30 days free, then a small daily fee. We’ll remind you before expiry.

What stores work? Any US store that ships to a US address.
