About QCS Cargo

QCS Cargo (Quiet Craft Solutions Cargo) connects the United States to the Caribbean with parcel forwarding and white-glove air freight. We give you a personal US address so you can shop any US store; we receive, store, and forward your packages to Guyana, Jamaica, Trinidad & Tobago, Barbados, and Suriname.

TSA licensed and fully insured. Based in Kearny, New Jersey.
