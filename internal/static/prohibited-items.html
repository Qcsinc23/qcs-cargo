Prohibited Items

Absolutely Prohibited — Never Accepted

  • ✕Firearms, ammunition, and weapons of any kind
  • ✕Explosives and pyrotechnics (fireworks, flares)
  • ✕Flammable liquids and gases (petrol, lighter fluid, aerosols)
  • ✕Illegal drugs and controlled narcotics
  • ✕Counterfeit goods and pirated materials
  • ✕Human remains
  • ✕Child pornography and obscene materials
  • ✕Currency, negotiable instruments exceeding $10,000

Restricted — Contact Us Before Shipping

  • !Lithium batteries (standalone, not in device) — require special handling
  • !Alcohol and spirits — destination customs restrictions apply
  • !Medications and medical devices — may require prescription documentation
  • !Seeds, plants, and agricultural products — phytosanitary certificate required
  • !Electronics exceeding $2,500 declared value
  • !Jewelry and watches over $1,000 declared value
  • !Food items — no perishables; sealed/packaged non-perishables may be accepted
  • !Laser pointers and stun guns — prohibited in some Caribbean destinations

Not Accepted for Operational Reasons

  • –Live animals and insects
  • –Live plants and flowers
  • –Perishable foods (fresh meat, dairy, produce)
  • –Single items over 150 lbs or exceeding 108 inches in any dimension
  • –Hazardous chemicals and acids
