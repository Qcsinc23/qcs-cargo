Shipping Policy

Transit Times

  • Standard Air: 3–5 business days (Guyana, Jamaica, Trinidad)
  • Standard Air: 4–6 business days (Barbados, Suriname)
  • Express Air: 1–2 business days (+25% surcharge)
  • Transit begins when ship request is confirmed and paid

Pricing

Rates per lb of billable weight = max(actual, dimensional). Dimensional = L×W×H (inches) ÷ 166. Volume discounts auto-applied above 100 lbs.

Customs & Duties

Customs clearance $35/shipment. Destination duties/taxes are recipient's responsibility. QCS prepares documentation from your declarations.

Package Receipt

Accepted Mon–Fri 8am–6pm EST. All packages photographed on arrival and matched to your suite code. You're notified immediately.

Damage & Loss

  • QCS assumes care from receipt to carrier handoff
  • Claims within 7 days of delivery
  • Reimbursement limited to declared/insured value
  • Not liable for carrier delays after handoff

Refused / Undeliverable

Packages refused by customs are returned to our warehouse. You bear re-shipment or disposal costs. Storage fees apply.

Insurance

Optional: declared_value ÷ 100 USD. Add when creating ship request. Claims require photo proof within 14 days.
