Privacy Policy

Quiet Craft Solutions Inc. ("QCS Cargo") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account info: name, email, phone
  • Shipping addresses and recipient details
  • Package data: weight, dimensions, photos, tracking numbers
  • Payment data processed via Stripe — we never store card numbers
  • Usage data: login times, browser type, IP address

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate our shipping and forwarding services
  • To send shipping notifications and storage alerts
  • To process payments and generate invoices
  • To comply with customs and export regulations
  • To detect and prevent fraud

3. Data Sharing

We share minimal data with: shipping carriers, Stripe (payments), Resend (email), and customs authorities as legally required. We do not sell your data.

4. Data Retention

Account data kept while active. Shipment records kept 7 years for compliance. Request deletion of non-compliance data anytime.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: request a copy of your data
  • Correction: update inaccurate profile information
  • Deletion: request account deletion (subject to legal retention)
  • Portability: export shipment history as CSV

6. Cookies & Security

Essential cookies only (httpOnly, secure) for authentication. No advertising cookies. HTTPS everywhere, bcrypt password hashing, JWT session tokens.

7. Contact

Privacy questions: Contact Us
