# QCS Cargo — User Guide

## For customers

**Sign up, get suite code, add packages to locker, create ship request, pay, track.**  
Sign up with your name and email to create an account. After verifying your email (magic link or password), you receive a **suite code** that identifies your locker at the warehouse. Use the locker view to add packages to your locker (tracking or manual entry). When ready, create a **ship request**: choose destination, add items, and pay via Stripe. You can then track the request and your packages from the dashboard.

## For warehouse staff

**Log in, locker receive, service queue, ship queue, receiving, staging.**  
Warehouse staff log in via the staff portal. Use **locker receive** to record packages arriving for a customer’s suite code. The **service queue** shows items awaiting processing; **ship queue** lists ship requests ready to pack and ship. Use **receiving** to log inbound shipments and **staging** to prepare outbound shipments. All actions update package and ship-request status for customers.

## For admins

**Dashboard, reports, users, settings.**  
Admins access the admin dashboard for an overview of lockers, ship requests, and revenue. Use **reports** for summaries by period, destination, or customer. **Users** lets you manage customer and staff accounts and roles. **Settings** cover system options, Stripe and email configuration, and other site-wide defaults.
